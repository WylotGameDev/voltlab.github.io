⚡ VOLTLAB v0.3

Physics-inspired electronics sandbox with 50+ components, realistic wiring, VFX and SFX.

Tools

1 SelectDrag parts, click Switch/Lever to toggle, Breaker to reset
2 PlacePick part from panel, click canvas to drop
3 WireClick terminal dot → click another terminal
4 DeleteClick part or wire to remove
5 WrenchClick burnt/exploded component to repair it
6 TouchTouch live/hot components — careful, you'll bleed!

Controls

RRotate selected component 90°
WCycle wire type
MMB dragPan camera
RMBDelete hovered item
DELDelete selected component
ESCCancel wire / deselect

Wire Types

CopperStandard — slight signal delay, reliable
GoldUltra-fast — max 0.15A only, burns out
SuperconductorZero resistance, fast propagation
Heat PipeConducts current AND heat to nearby parts
RopeNon-conductive, structural / decorative

Hazards

Overvoltage causes components to burn or explode. Gold wire burns out at high current. Use the Wrench to repair damage. The Touch tool lets you physically interact — live or hot surfaces damage your HP!
